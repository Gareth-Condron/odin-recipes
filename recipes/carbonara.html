Pasta Carbonara

Description

A delicious dish of pasta carbonara! Made with a creamy sauce, this dish conatains ham, chicken and occasionally mushrooms.

A simple and easy to make meal that is very filling and incredibly tasty!

Ingredients

  • Ham or Bacon bits
  • Chicken Breast or Chicken Thighs
  • Carbonara Sauce
  • Pasta
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Place the pasta in a saucepan with water. Add heat.
  2. Chop the chicken into small pieces
  3. Cook the chicken on a frying pan.
  4. When the pasta is softened sufficiently reduce heat.
  5. Add the bacon bits or ham to chicken to cook, then add the sauce.
  6. Serve all ingredients togther in a bowl.
