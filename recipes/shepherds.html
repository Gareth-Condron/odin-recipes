Shepherd's Pie

Description

A classic recipe, this is sure to warm you up on cold winter days!

Can be made with a variety of ingredients, here is just one example.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Minced beef or lamb

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes and carrots.
  2. Fry minced beef/lamb.
  3. Mix carrots and mince in a glass dish.Place mashed potatoes in a layer over the mince.
